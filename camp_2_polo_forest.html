Road Trip & Backpacking To Himachal


7 Days | 12 Locations | SUV Road Trip | Backpacking | Paragliding | Rafting | 2500 kms | Winter | 4 States | North


Road trip to Himachal(Extreme Winter), driving through valleys, peaks, high mountains, snows, lakes, woods, rough terrains to explore exotic locations by our SUV for 7 days , yes its backpacking road trip. Sounds like your Cup of Tea?


We are foodies and yes we are adventure freaks. We are not gonna book a lodge or star hotels but yes we carry our tents and sleeping bags and yes we gonna roll in our SUV.


Bucket list : white water rafting, paragliding, camping everywhere are in our list too.


Places we gonna explore : Agra, Dharmshala, Mcleodganj, Bir Biling, Pershar Lake, Kullu, Manikaran, Kasol, Manali

FAQs

  • QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

  • 1) Is it safe?
    It’s 100% safe and kindly note it’s 100% adventurous.

  • 2) Which vehicle we are using for road trip?
    We are renting SUV preferably xuv500 or Scorpio.

  • 3) How safe it is for solo women travellers?
    As we told earlier, its 100% adventurous, backpacking road trip & our team will ensure 100% safety for all our Co-travellers.

  • 4) How will be the weather?
    The weather will be cold and it’s highly advisable to wear thermal wears & proper winter clothing.

  • 5) Why food is not included?
    As we will be travelling continuous for 7 days we will be stopping by motels based on availability & co-travellers decision. However, the food will be cheap & be prepared as you won’t find briyani over there :p

  • 6) What are the adventures activities we can cover?
    You can try paragliding at Birbiling (Asia’s highest paragliding spot). Rafting at Kulu & Manali. It may cost you Rs.2500 for paragliding & Rs.1000 for rafting.

  • 7) What happens in case the car breaks down or got repaired?
    Ha Ha, Dude get ready to push the car, board the local bus, Walk for miles or whatever it takes to keep going.

  • 8) Where we stay?
    Mostly it will be a camping & budget room stay.

  • 9) Will trekking be hard?
    Nope, we don’t want to make you tired.
